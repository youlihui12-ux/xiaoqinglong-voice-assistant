## Forum / Community Post (V2EX, Reddit)

### Title
[Showcase] Xiaoqinglong: Transform your Mac into a voice-controlled AI workstation

### Body
Xiaoqinglong is a "bridge" project that connects voice entry (Xiaozhi/MCP) with local execution (macOS/LobeHub).

**Why I built this:**
I wanted a voice assistant that didn't just tell me the weather, but could actually *do* things on my Mac—like opening a specific project in VS Code, summarizing an Obsidian note, or cleaning up my desktop—all while keeping my data on my machine.

**Key Features:**
- **Xiaozhi MCP Bridge:** Low-latency WebSocket connection for voice commands.
- **LobeHub Dispatcher:** Uses LobeHub's local agents as the "brain" for reasoning.
- **Mission Control UI:** A browser dashboard to see what's happening in real-time.
- **Safety First:** Built-in app allowlists and manual approval for sensitive commands.

Looking for feedback and contributors!
